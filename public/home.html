About This Project

Welcome to our crime prediction platform, designed to analyze and forecast crime patterns in New York City. The goal of this project is to use advanced data analytics and machine learning techniques to predict whether a crime will happen based on historical crime data.

By examining factors like time, location, gender , age . we aim to provide insights into potential crime hotspots and help authorities make informed decisions about resource allocation, safety measures, and crime prevention strategies. Our system takes into account various features, such as:

  • Time of day: Whether the crime occurs during the day, night, or in specific time frames.
  • Location: Geospatial data identifying areas with high crime occurrences.
  • Historical patterns: Identifying trends and repeating cycles from previous crime data.

With this platform, we aim to assist law enforcement agencies, policymakers, and the general public in understanding crime patterns, improving safety, and ensuring a more secure environment for residents and visitors of New York City.
